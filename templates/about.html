About Me

My journey commenced at a young age when I received my first IBM computer, igniting my fascination with the terminal and its potential for action and interaction. This curiosity has been a constant companion, leading me to pursue my initial degree in systems engineering at the Universidad de Oriente (UDO). Having moved from Venezuela to the United States in 2017, I embraced the role of a software engineer at Relevance Lab. In this role, I acquired hands-on experience with a variety of tools and languages, delving into the different sides of DevOps methodology, REST API development, CI/CD, server configuration management, containerization, and bootstrap scripting.

Beyond my professional experience, what sets me apart is my strong ability to learn quickly and teach myself new concepts and technologies. I've demonstrated this through my self-learning journey, which includes mastering three different languages, teaching myself how to play the piano and guitar, and acquiring new coding languages and tools independently.
